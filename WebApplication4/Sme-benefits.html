How UoT Works

Fast, Easily and Efficiently.

Starting your path to consulting success and satisfaction couldn’t be easier. Here’s how it works:

Join the UoT Community:

Start by completing your professional UoT profile.

Businesses find you:

Businesses with specific project requirements search on UoT for the right subject matter expert. If that’s you, the business sends a request asking you to respond to the project listing.

Negotiate your terms:

You respond to the request with an invitation to discuss the project securely within the UoT Marketplace. There, you discuss the scope, deliverables and pricing for moving forward.

Receive your project payment:

UoT makes weekly payments into your UoT account as your project progresses or upon your project’s completion.
